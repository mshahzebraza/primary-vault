# Vault audit — 2026-06-24

A full read-only health pass over the 158-note vault: metadata, link graph, meeting linkage, manifest hygiene, and supersession. Nothing was edited.

**Headline:** the vault is healthy in content but **leaky in connections**. The single biggest issue is exactly what the new `resolve-meeting` skill targets — **14 of 21 meeting notes have zero inbound links**, meaning their action items were captured and then never wired into a feature doc or manifest. Most of the rest is low-severity reference drift and by-design "unresolved" type labels that only *look* alarming until you separate them out.

This report sorts findings into **fix now** (real bugs), **structural drift**, and **decisions** (systemic graph gaps that are choices, not errors). The three graph-gap decisions are now settled — see Decisions at the bottom.
