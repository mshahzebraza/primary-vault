# Projects

Use **`[[Projects]]`** in `categories` for work that tracks a **product, app, repo, or initiative** under an organization.

- Pair with **`[[Work]]`** and an org hub (e.g. `[[aiquery.io]]`).
- For R&D or product-spec notes, `sync-categories` may add `[[Projects]]` automatically when `path_area` is **R&D** or **Docs**.
- Add dedicated hubs (e.g. a note named like the product) and link them in `categories` when you want finer grouping.
